Transportation

Arriving on Taniti

By Air The primary way to reach Taniti. The island has a small airport served by charter and regional flights. Most visitors arrive by air.
By Cruise Ship Cruise ships dock at Taniti's port on a weekly basis. Cruise passengers typically visit for the day.

Getting Around the Island

Public Buses Bus service operates within Taniti City and to some surrounding areas. An affordable option for short distances.
Taxis Available throughout Taniti City. Recommended for door-to-door travel, especially after dark.
Rental Cars Available for rent — visitors must be 21 or older with a valid license. Gives the most flexibility for exploring the full island.
Bike Rentals Bicycles are available to rent in Taniti City. Good for exploring the local area at a leisurely pace.
On Foot Taniti City is walkable. Many restaurants, shops, and attractions are within easy walking distance of central lodging.
