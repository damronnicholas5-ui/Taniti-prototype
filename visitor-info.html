Visitor Information

Essential Travel Information

  • Currency The U.S. dollar is the accepted currency on Taniti. Most businesses accept cash; credit card acceptance varies by vendor.
  • Safety Taniti is generally considered safe for tourists. Exercise standard travel precautions: secure your valuables, stay aware of surroundings, and avoid poorly lit areas after dark. Emergency services are available in Taniti City.
  • Power / Electricity Taniti uses 120-volt outlets, the same standard as the United States. No adapter is needed for U.S. travelers.
  • Alcohol Alcohol may be purchased and consumed between 9:00 a.m. and 10:00 p.m. only. Bars and restaurants must follow these hours.
  • Public Holidays Some businesses and attractions may be closed or have reduced hours on local holidays. Confirm hours of any planned activities before your visit.
  • Language English is widely spoken in tourist areas. Most signs and menus in Taniti City are available in English.
  • Health & Medical Basic medical services are available on the island. Travelers are encouraged to bring any prescription medications needed for the duration of their stay.
