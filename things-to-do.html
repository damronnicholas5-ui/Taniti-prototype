Things to Do

Outdoor & Nature

Beaches & Snorkeling Relax on white-sand beaches or explore the reef. Snorkeling gear is available for rent at several beach locations.
Rainforest Adventures Guided hikes and nature tours through Taniti's interior rainforest. Zip-lining also available.
Volcano Tours Scenic viewpoints and guided excursions to Taniti's active volcano. Tours depart from Taniti City.

Cultural & Entertainment

Local History Museum Learn about the island's culture and heritage. Open most days — confirm hours on arrival (may close on local holidays).
Restaurants & Dining Local and American-style restaurants throughout Taniti City. Grocery stores also available for self-catering travelers.
Nightlife Bars and a small bowling alley in Taniti City. Note: alcohol is only sold between 9 a.m. and 10 p.m.
