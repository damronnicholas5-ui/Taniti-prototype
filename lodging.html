Lodging

All lodging options on Taniti are regulated and inspected by the local government. Contact individual properties for current availability and pricing.

Lodging Options

Hotels & Resorts Full-service accommodations popular with leisure travelers. Most are located near Taniti City and the beaches. Amenities typically include restaurants, pools, and tour desks.
Bed & Breakfasts Smaller, locally owned stays offering a more personal experience. Good choice for travelers seeking local character.
Hostels Budget-friendly dormitory or private room options. Ideal for solo travelers or those keeping costs low.
Family-Owned Lodging Locally run guesthouses offering personalized service. Prices and amenities vary — contact hosts directly.

Planning Tips

  • Book Early Tourism is growing on Taniti; availability can be limited during peak seasons.
  • Location Most lodging is concentrated in and around Taniti City, close to activities and transport.
  • Currency U.S. dollars are accepted. Confirm payment methods with your lodging provider.
